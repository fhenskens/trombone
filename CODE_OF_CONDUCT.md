# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We want this project to be welcoming and harassment-free for everyone.

## Our Standards

Examples of behavior that contributes to a positive environment include:

- Being kind and respectful.
- Giving helpful feedback.
- Accepting feedback from others.
- Taking responsibility for mistakes.
- Doing what is best for the community.

Examples of unacceptable behavior include:

- Sexual language or unwanted sexual attention.
- Trolling, insults, or personal attacks.
- Public or private harassment.
- Sharing private information without permission.
- Other behavior that is not appropriate in a professional community.

## Enforcement Responsibilities

Maintainers are responsible for enforcing this code.
They may take fair action on behavior that is inappropriate, threatening, or harmful.

## Scope

This code applies in all project spaces and when representing the project in public.

## Enforcement

Instances of abusive, harassing, or otherwise unacceptable behavior may be reported to the maintainers at `conduct@trombone.dev`.
All reports will be reviewed promptly and fairly.
